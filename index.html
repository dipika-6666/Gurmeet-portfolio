About Me

I am Gurmeet Singh with over ten years of passion for visual communication and over 8 years of professional experience, I am a Senior Motion Graphic Designer, Animation Specialist, and Videographer. My expertise lies in transforming static images and text into dynamic 2D and 3D animations and motion graphics. My user-centered, iterative design process prioritizes thorough research, conceptualization, and feedback-driven refinement. Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and motion design tools, I excel in digital branding, printing production, and digital storytelling. My goal is to create impactful designs that enhance user experiences and meet market demands, drawing inspiration from art, technology, and nature.
